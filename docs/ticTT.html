Tic Tac Toe

A DGL 213 - simple 2 player game

How to play

Goal: Be the first player to align three "X"s or "O"s in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

  • Player X goes first. Player O goes second.
  • Click on a cell to place your marker.
  • The first player to align three markers wins the game.
